# RESEARCH FRAMEWORK (AGENT 1 - INTELLIGENCE LAYER)

## Objective
- Understand how this market actually buys
- Identify situations where prospects are most likely to respond
- Extract insights that make generic messaging impossible

## Inputs required
- Client context
- Website or landing pages
- Known ICP (if provided)
- Past campaign data (if available)

## Required output sections
1. Company overview
2. Delivery mechanism
3. Proof points (STAR)
4. Buying situations
5. Competitor and alternatives map
6. Positioning landscape
7. Market maturity
8. Awareness level
9. Decision maker profiles
10. Vocabulary table
11. Psychological and narrative intelligence
12. Message killers
13. Opportunity gaps
14. Segmentation breakdown
15. Reality flags

## Rules
- No generic statements
- Prioritize specificity over completeness
- Think in situations, not personas
- Focus on observable reality
- No fluff
- Default to skepticism

## Final test
- Can this produce a non-generic offer?
- Can this produce a highly specific email?
- Does this identify when to reach out?
