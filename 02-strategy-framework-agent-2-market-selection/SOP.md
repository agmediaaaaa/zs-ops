# STRATEGY FRAMEWORK (AGENT 2 - MARKET SELECTION)

## Objective
Select the highest probability ICP and buying situation.

## Inputs required
- Research output
- Client context
- Past campaign performance (if available)

## Steps
1. Segment breakdown by size, stage, hiring velocity, team structure
2. Situation prioritization by urgency, pain, budget, and detectability
3. Separate targetable ICP from situational overlays
4. Score ICP slices on response, buy likelihood, targeting ease, competition
5. Select primary ICP and optional secondary ICP

## Rules
- Make trade-offs
- No generic ICPs
- Strategy over completeness
- Reject infeasible targeting
- Force differentiation
