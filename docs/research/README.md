# Research methodology

Research standards, methodology, and how we do our research.

## ICP Training

**[ICP Training — Outbound GTM & Lead Development](./ICP_TRAINING.md)** — SOP / operational playbook that combines lead list development, GTM research, signal-based targeting, offer discovery, and outbound campaign creation. Covers market intelligence, buying situation mapping, signal identification, ICP segmentation, list building, stealth offers, campaign creation, messaging, testing, and feedback loops. Use for building a repeatable outbound engine.

## Outbound Offer Testing Playbook (Scaling / Testing)

**[Outbound Offer Testing Playbook](./OUTBOUND_OFFER_TESTING_PLAYBOOK.md)** — Method for systematically testing offer angles across ICP segments and scaling winners. Covers: two-track system (Signal/Intent vs ICP Offer Testing), 3 ICPs × 5 offers × 1000–1500 leads per campaign, lead list rules, 5–7 day evaluation window, reply/positive-reply/meeting metrics, winning-offer selection, scaling path, and iteration cycle. Use for removing guesswork and letting market response drive positioning.

## ICP Mapping System

**[ICP Mapping System](./ICP_MAPPING_SYSTEM.md)** — Framework for designing ICPs for high-volume outbound. Optimizes for segment-level targeting, simple filters, and 7.5–8/10 relevance at scale (not hyper-personalization). Covers: role cluster development, phase-based pain mapping, phase-driven buying moment model, scalable targeting signals (Apollo/Sales Nav/Clay), list-building filter logic, pain-qualified campaign buckets, campaign brief output, and outbound-efficiency prioritization. Input: TAM analysis + onboarding form. Output: min 9 segment-role combinations, filters, campaign-ready buckets, ranked prioritization.

## Lead Scraping Module

**[Lead Scraping Module](./LEAD_SCRAPING_MODULE.md)** — Two-part module for scalable B2B prospecting data. **Part 1 — Lead Intelligence:** target specs, scalable signal model (Tier 1/2), source mapping (10–15 sources), platform role specialization (Primary/Secondary/Optional), filter structures for Apollo/Sales Nav/Clay, cost-benefit analysis, strategic sourcing plan, quality control, execution roadmap. **Part 2 — Keyword Architecture:** role cluster, industry, phase/operational, solution category, and pain-theme keyword pools; Boolean-ready strings; Broad / Precision / Ultra search configurations; match rate estimation. Optimizes for high volume, low Clay burn, 2026 deliverability-safe operations.
